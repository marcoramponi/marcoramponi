About Me

Full CV (pdf)

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin, in the group of Algebraic Geometry. Previously, I held a postdoc position at Universität Augsburg. I obtained my PhD in Mathematics at Université de Poitiers. I graduated with a BSc in Mathematics and a MSc in Mathematics at Universitá degli Studi di Milano.
