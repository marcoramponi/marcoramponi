Research

Research field and interests

  • My research field is algebraic geometry. My study is focused on topics related to:
    Linear systems and vector bundles on algebraic curves or surfaces, Brill-Noether theory
    for curves on surfaces, in particular K3 surfaces, and applications to moduli spaces.
  • All my research papers and preprints are available and updated on [my arXiv page].
